Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) - Crop Insurance Guide

OVERVIEW
PMFBY provides comprehensive risk coverage for all stages of crop cycle including pre-sowing to post-harvest losses due to non-preventable natural risks.

PREMIUM RATES
- Kharif crops: 2% of sum insured (maximum)
- Rabi crops: 1.5% of sum insured (maximum)
- Annual commercial and horticultural crops: 5% of sum insured (maximum)
- Remaining premium paid by Central and State Governments equally

COVERAGE
Risks covered:
- Natural fire, lightning, storm, hailstorm, cyclone, typhoon, flood, inundation, landslide
- Drought, dry spells, pest/disease attack
- Post-harvest losses for up to 14 days from harvest

Risks NOT covered:
- War and nuclear risks
- Malicious damage, theft
- Other preventable risks

SUM INSURED
- Based on Scale of Finance fixed by District Level Technical Committee (DLTC)
- Covers actual cost of cultivation

CLAIM PROCESS
1. Crop loss must be more than 25% to trigger claim
2. Report crop loss within 72 hours via crop insurance app or 14447 helpline
3. Joint survey by insurance company, state government, bank
4. Claim settlement within 2 months of harvest completion

ENROLLMENT
- Loanee farmers: Automatic enrollment through banks (can opt-out)
- Non-loanee farmers: Enroll at nearest bank, insurance company branch, or online
- Deadline: 1-2 weeks before sowing for Kharif; as notified for Rabi

HELPLINE: 14447 (toll-free)
PORTAL: pmfby.gov.in
