Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme - Complete Information

PURPOSE
KCC provides adequate and timely credit support to farmers for their agricultural needs including purchase of inputs like seeds, fertilizers, pesticides and also for allied activities.

INTEREST RATE
- Concessional rate: 7% per annum
- Prompt repayment incentive: Additional 3% interest subvention
- Effective rate of 4% per annum for timely repayment
- For loans up to Rs 3 lakh against agriculture

REPAYMENT PERIOD
- Crop loans: Within 12 months (aligned with harvest/marketing season)
- Term loans: 5 years
- KCC validity: 5 years with annual review

ELIGIBILITY
- All farmers including individual/joint borrowers
- Tenant farmers, oral lessees, share croppers
- Self Help Groups/Joint Liability Groups of farmers
- Minimum age 18 years; Maximum age 75 years

APPLICATION PROCESS
1. Visit nearest bank (cooperative, commercial, RRB)
2. Fill KCC application form
3. Submit land records, ID proof, photograph
4. Bank assesses credit limit based on land holding and crops

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Identity proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID)
- Land ownership/tenancy documents
- Passport size photographs
- Application form

KCC FOR ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND FISHERIES
- Extended to allied sector farmers
- Dairy: Rs 3 lakh per unit
- Fisheries: Rs 2 lakh for inland fishing
- Poultry: Rs 50,000 per unit
